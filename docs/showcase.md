## How to add your entry

1. Open a PR.
2. Add a line to the table below with:
   - **Name** — your company or project name.
   - **Use case** — one sentence describing the production use.
   - **Reference** — optional link to a blog post, talk, or doc.
3. We require confirmation that you are authorized to make the
   public claim (a public-facing blog post, a published case study,
   or a maintainer review is enough).

## Disclaimer

This page is opt-in. atemporal is an open-source project, and we
respect the privacy of our users. Entries are removed on request
by filing an issue with the title "Showcase: please remove
*Company Name*".
